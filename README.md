# PredictiveAnalysisSem2
Codes and Dataset for MDTS4214

## Field of Study
Automobile Industry Analytics

## Dataset Description
This dataset contains information related to the used car market, where each record represents a single car along with its selling price and key specifications. The data includes vehicle characteristics, usage details, fuel type, transmission, ownership history, and performance-related attributes. The dataset is intended for predictive analytics tasks, specifically for predicting car prices based on these features.

## Target Variable
- **selling_price**: The price at which the car was sold.

## Features
- **year**: Manufacturing year of the car  
- **km_driven**: Total distance driven by the car  
- **fuel**: Type of fuel used (Petrol, Diesel, CNG, Electric)  
- **seller_type**: Type of seller (Individual, Dealer, Trustmark Dealer)  
- **transmission**: Manual or Automatic transmission  
- **owner**: Ownership history of the car  
- **mileage(km/ltr/kg)**: Fuel efficiency of the car  
- **engine**: Engine capacity of the car  
- **max_power**: Maximum power output of the car  
- **seats**: Seating capacity of the car  

## Source
The dataset is taken from Kaggle
